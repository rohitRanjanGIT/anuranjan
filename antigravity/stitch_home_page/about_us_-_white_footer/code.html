Our Legacy & Story

Anuranjan Infratech Resources Pvt Ltd was founded with a vision to revolutionize the infrastructure sector. Over the years, we have grown from a small consultancy into a multi-disciplinary engineering and construction powerhouse.

Our journey is defined by a relentless pursuit of quality and a commitment to delivering complex projects on time. We take pride in our heritage of engineering excellence and our role in building the nation's backbone through sustainable and innovative infrastructure solutions.
